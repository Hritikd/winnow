# Benchmark results

Generated by `python benchmarks/bench.py --write`. Deterministic; uses the dependency-free heuristic tokenizer so anyone reproduces the same numbers.

**Gold recall** = fraction of human-labeled relevant chunks that survive compression to the given token budget (higher is better). Dataset: `benchmarks/data.json` (8 retrieval cases where the relevant passages are *scattered*, not at the front).

| method | gold recall @ 25% budget | gold recall @ 40% budget | gold recall @ 60% budget |
|---|---|---|---|
| **winnow** (relevance + diversity) | 0.38 | 0.75 | 0.88 |
| bm25 (relevance only) | 0.38 | 0.75 | 0.88 |
| head-truncation (naive default) | 0.00 | 0.19 | 0.44 |
| random | 0.19 | 0.38 | 0.44 |
